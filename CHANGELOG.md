# Changelog

## v3.6.2 — 2026-07-03

Polished the live lyrics: the current line stands out more, already-sung lines fade into a soft trail instead of a wall of colour, and lines dissolve smoothly at the edges.

## v3.6.1 — 2026-07-03

Album covers now work for songs in any language (Russian, Japanese, etc.) — a bug was blocking non-English titles. Missing covers on your existing top songs get filled in automatically too.

## v3.6.0 — 2026-07-03

Renamed Friends to Following (it's a one-way follow), and profiles now show a follower count.

## v3.5.1 — 2026-07-03

Hotfix: v3.5.0 could fail to start on some installs (a missing bundled module). Fixed.

## v3.5.0 — 2026-07-03

New: Friends. Follow people from their profile or the leaderboard, then see what everyone you follow is playing right now — live, in one feed. Vinyl is a social music app now.

## v3.4.0 — 2026-07-03

Under-the-hood: downloads now count real new installs only (auto-updates no longer inflate the number).

## v3.3.0 — 2026-07-03

Browser detection now only mirrors real music sites - no more random web audio showing up. SoundCloud is recognized instantly. Live 'listening right now' cards on profiles refresh much faster.

## v3.2.0 — 2026-07-03

The leaderboard is now the Weekly Top 50 - a fresh race every week. Fixed a bug where one listen could count as two or three plays.

## v3.1.0 — 2026-07-02

Beatdrops pump now - sustained loud sections can't pin the lights into one bright blob. New visualizer settings: Punch, Brightness, and a colour-shift-on-the-beat toggle.

## v3.0.0 — 2026-07-02

Vinyl 3.0 - the visualizer got real. The lights and record lock onto the app your music is actually coming from, and a new beat engine makes kicks genuinely hit, even on loud modern masters.

## v2.52.0 — 2026-07-02

Profiles glow in the colors of whatever that person is playing right now (or their top song). The spinning vinyl on Discord blends into the profile card. Cleaner settings, and bug reports from the Feedback section.

## v2.51.0 — 2026-07-02

Live profiles: see what anyone is listening to right now - spinning cover, live progress bar, and an ambient tint from their album art. Live EQ dots on the Top 50. New privacy toggle in Settings.
