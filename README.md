## What it does

- **Real Rich Presence** — your Discord profile shows the song with a live
  seek bar, spinning album-art vinyl, and the platform's own name and logo
  (Spotify, Apple Music, SoundCloud, YouTube Music, TIDAL, Deezer, Amazon
  Music). Custom buttons can open the song or your music profile.
- **Live synced lyrics** — time-synced lyrics scroll with the track in the
  app and can show on your status line by line.
- **Music profiles** — badges, top artists and songs, listening streaks, and
  a live "listening right now" card your friends can open in one click.
- **Weekly Top 50** — a fresh leaderboard race every week for who listened
  the most.
- **Audio-reactive visualizer** — background lights and the record lock onto
  the app your music is coming from and pump with the beat.

Works with Spotify **Free or Premium** and every other player — Vinyl reads
playback from Windows itself, so there's nothing to connect or sign into.

## Install

Download [VinylSetup.exe](https://vinyl.bigg.dev/VinylSetup.exe) and run it —
no admin rights needed. If Windows notes the app is new, click
**More info → Run anyway**.

Or, once the winget package is approved:

```
winget install vinyl
```

Every release is also attached on the [Releases](../../releases) page, with
the full history in [CHANGELOG.md](CHANGELOG.md).

## Privacy

No accounts, no ads, no trackers. Sharing (leaderboard minutes, profile,
live song) is opt-out in Settings. Full details: [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md).
